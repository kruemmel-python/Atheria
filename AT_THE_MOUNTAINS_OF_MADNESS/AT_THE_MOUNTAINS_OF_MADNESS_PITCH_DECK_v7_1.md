## Slide 01 - AT THE MOUNTAINS OF MADNESS

Hollywood Pitch Deck v7.1 | Final Package
Cosmic Horror Thriller | Premium Studio Feature

- Tagline: The oldest intelligence on Earth is not hunting us. It is evaluating us.
- Final Identity: Engineer-intelligence, human iteration, revelation as horror.

## Slide 02 - The Hook

- A rescue mission enters Antarctica and finds an active system, not a ruin.
- The deeper they go, the clearer it becomes: humanity may be one version in a designed cycle.
- The film sells dread through discovery, not creature attack patterns.

## Slide 03 - Logline

- A grieving systems scientist leads a polar recovery team into a buried pre-human complex and discovers that Earth has been repeatedly reset.
- To bring anyone home, he must choose between exposing the truth and containing it, while evidence suggests he may already be integrated into the evaluating intelligence.

## Slide 04 - Story Summary

- Camp D1 goes silent; a six-person team arrives to retrieve data and personnel.
- Inside the camp, evidence points to precise biological investigation, not random violence.
- Below the ice they discover an iteration chamber with preserved human and near-human variants.
- Keller realizes the core horror: civilization may be a test environment repeatedly reset.
- A structural collapse removes the return route. Escape requires moving deeper before moving out.
- The Elder Thing completes its analysis and enters the human world. The cycle remains active.

## Slide 05 - 3-Act Spine

- Act I: Recovery mission -> active architecture -> weather clock -> descent decision.
- Act II: Iteration evidence -> emotional fracture -> revelation of reset logic.
- Act III: No-return escape -> institutional cover -> mirrored ending confirms recurrence.
